OISÍN

IXD Design

about

Oisín is a UX designer built and bred in Belfast, he has a strong eye for detail and passion for creation, travel and good food. He graduated in 2014 with a foundation degree in Interactive Media and now he is a current student at the University of Ulster studying Interaction Design.

"I want to make beautiful things, even if nobody cares." -Saul Bass

He has a particular interest in Branding , And is fascinated by the process of being able to subconsciously appeal to certain audiences using design. Starting from creating logos to developing all other aspects of the brand. He aims to create a consistent and strong brand to improve the business.
